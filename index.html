The Buzz

A timeless room behind the wavy wall

Tucked on a quiet corner of Catalpa Avenue in Andersonville, Wil’s is a small, dark, art-forward room built for slow nights and cold martinis. Copper light, tufted leather, a pressed-tin ceiling, and the abstract mural that gives the whole place its pulse. Come as a regular, leave as family.

Owned by Wil & Charlie

More than a bar. A home with better lighting.

With over 20 years in hospitality, Wil Mcerlean, along with his husband Charlie Bonanno, are the visionaries behind Wil’s. The goal was to create more than a bar — to build a timeless space where every drink is a work of art and guests feel at home.

You’ll usually find them here: Wil behind the bar, Charlie working the room, both carrying on the Andersonville martini tradition one cold glass at a time.
